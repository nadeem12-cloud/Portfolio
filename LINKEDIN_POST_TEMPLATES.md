## 📝 Quick Tips for LinkedIn Posts

### What Works:
- **Hook in first line** — Make them stop scrolling
- **Break up text** — Use emojis, bullets, line breaks
- **Show, don't just tell** — Numbers, metrics, specifics
- **Call to action** — What do you want readers to do?
- **Hashtags (5-8 max)** — Mix popular + niche tags

### What Doesn't:
- ❌ Wall of text (people skim)
- ❌ Generic phrases ("passionate about tech")
- ❌ Begging for likes/comments
- ❌ Too many hashtags (looks spammy)
- ❌ Overpromising without proof

### Engagement Tactics:
1. **Post timing:** 8-10 AM or 5-7 PM IST (when professionals check LinkedIn)
2. **Ask questions:** "What features would you add?"
3. **Tag connections:** "@Name thanks for the feedback during building this"
4. **Respond to comments:** Engage within first hour
5. **Share in stories:** Repost to LinkedIn stories for extra reach

## 🎯 Featured Section Setup

1. Go to LinkedIn profile
2. Scroll to **Featured** section
3. Click **+Add featured**
4. Select **Link**
5. Fill in:
   - **URL:** Your portfolio link
   - **Title:** "My Developer Portfolio - ML & Full-Stack Projects"
   - **Description:** Pick one:

**Option A (Professional):**
```
A showcase of my work in Machine Learning, Data Science, and Full-Stack Development. Features deployed projects including an IPL match predictor with explainable AI, NLP-powered job search engine, and a modern poetry platform. Built with Python, React, XGBoost, and cloud deployment expertise.
```

**Option B (Direct):**
```
Portfolio showcasing real ML and full-stack projects — all deployed, all open-source. IPL predictor with SHAP explainability, job search with semantic matching, React poetry platform. No templates. No tutorials. Just shipped code.
```
